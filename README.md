# Biofall

> **Containment has failed. The city is collapsing.**
> A fast-paced top-down shooter where a lone operative fights through a quarantined metropolis overrun by bio-organic horrors.

---

## Overview

**Genre:** Top-Down Action Shooter
**Players:** 1 (Single-player) *(co-op as stretch goal)*
**Engine:** Unreal Engine
**Platforms:** PC

A classified bioengineering experiment has triggered a catastrophic outbreak in a sealed city. The infection mutates organic matter into aggressive, rapidly evolving entities.

You are part of a last-response unit sent into the quarantine zone — not to save the city, but to **contain what's left of it**.

---

## Gameplay Loop

Deploy → Explore Level → Engage Enemies → Complete Objectives → Extract → Upgrade → Next Mission

---

## Objectives

- Survive waves
- Reach extraction point
- Activate systems (generators, scanners)
- Seal contamination zones
- Eliminate priority targets

---

## Progression

**Currency:** Bio Samples / Credits
**Unlocks:**

- Weapons (pistol → rifle → heavy)
- Upgrades:
  - Damage
  - Fire rate
  - Max health
  - Mobility

Optional perk system for passive bonuses. Weapon and upgrade data is defined in Data Assets for fully data-driven tuning.

---

## Narrative Design

Minimalist, gameplay-first storytelling.

**Delivery:**

- Mission briefings
- Radio transmissions
- Environmental clues

**Story Arc:**

- Early: containment attempt
- Mid: system collapse
- Late: origin discovery

---

## MVP Scope

- 1 level (urban quarantine zone)
- 1 player character
- 3–4 weapons
- 4 enemy types + 1 mini-boss
- Basic wave system
- Simple objective (survive & extract)
- Core UI (HP, ammo, wave)
